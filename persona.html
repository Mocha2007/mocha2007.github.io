ASL Religion Language MBTI OCEAN Kinsey Fav. Subject
#(#(#)) a abc abc* abcn abcd ##### # abcn

An Explanation

All categories are separated by slashes or bars. Any category may be ommitted by replacing the slot with a hash (#).

ASL

As they say on Omegle, "ASL?". As annoying as the phrase is, it is indeed very good for getting the basic stats on a person. Age is given in years. Gender is a single character - the first letter of the gender. Generally, this will mean f or m. And yes, this position uses gender, not sex. If you are transfemale or transmale you prefix the f/m with a capital T. Note this only applies to those two specifically - otherwise it is redundant. Location is a three-letter code following ISO 3166-1 alpha-3.

Language

Native language, followed by fluent languages in ISO 639-3, comma separated. Can use plus or minus to indicate fluency.

Personality Indicators

Round the percentage to the nearest ten and use the first digit of that, eg. 93->9 or 27->2. If rounding would cause it to exceed 90 then use 9 instead.

Favorites

eg. CHM = chemistry, etc. Can be chained with commas, ordered by like. Can prefix a subject by ! to indicate dislikes.
