Jupiter

Statistic Value
Length 216 words
22 sentences
54 seconds
%1000 94%
Hardest words geyser, probe, scientist, planet, fifth

Jupiter is the fifth planet of our sun. It is the largest of all eight known planets. It is mostly made of gas, and so it is called a gas giant. It is brown, cold, and very pretty.

Jupiter's most interesting feature is its large red storm, called the great red spot. It is a storm that has existed for hundreds of years, and is larger than even the Earth!

Jupiter has four large moons, but many more small ones. The first of these moons, Io, looks very strange. It is white and yellow, with spots of many other colors. It has many geyser which send water very high up!

Jupiter's second large moon is Europa. It is a ball of ice, with many cuts along its surface. Many scientists think it may have a sea under its surface!

Jupiter's third large moon is Ganymede. It is a large grey ball of rock larger than even Mercury!

Jupiter's last large moon is Callisto. It is another strange looking moon. It looks like a black ball with many white points.

Right now, Jupiter is being studied by NASA's Juno probe. The probe studies Jupiter's clouds up close. Many pretty pictures have come from Juno!

Maybe one day we will be able to visit our largest planet, Jupiter!
