Gaba Language

I made this language since I was curious how a language influenced by lambda calculus would look. Nothing too new or exciting, but I prefer the grammar of this language over lojban, at least. Not designed to be practical, just designed to be usable.
  • VSO
  • Three "pronouns": i, a, and u: I, you, and that.
  • Words pa means the next word is treated as a verb
  • The leftmost word is read and treated like a verb, reading the next n words as its arguments.
  • (C)V(n/s/z)

Samples

  • ban i a. - I love you.
  • ruz u. - This [thing] is red.
  • na i pa duz ga. - I want a red person.
  • na i pa ba a i pa ruz ga. - Who is the red person?
  • du pa ruz ga Tom. - The red person is Tom.

English words which are compounds in Gaba

  • sa tin - day, daytime
  • as tin, nu pa sa tin - night, nighttime
  • kudi tin - now
