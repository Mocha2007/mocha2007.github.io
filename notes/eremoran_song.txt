eremoran artform: bonrôm

----- intro -----

ho hakiz da bo afkaz
going through this confusing land

dir dim din ittô saliz
I meet unfamiliar faces

rômi i monar ne, anoz?
who am I, I wonder?

"skitômz defôz" bo rôboz
"we can absolutely show you", they insist

dimok i rômi dô samarz
now something lies inside me

ad tukilk dô tukirôz
It whispers into my mind

dindôkaz dô i sazaz
I fear what I'm becoming

dir tukilk nasdôkaz i banz
I feel my mind splitting in two

tukilk dô afik i rankenz
the beast inside me rises

ho eba din berbatamaz
I must escape this body

ho eba din berbatamaz
I must escape this body

----- pre-chorus -----

henlôm ad asuz
I totally freeze

tônkad i hanuz
I hold my stomach

tukilk i abubz
my mind turns

afik i saurz
I see the beast

hom bam i amum
this freedom is sweet

----- chorus -----

ad ôkarinum beka su
a road to insanity

kesum henlôm su sedat su
or a field of divine truth (alt: health)

afik i ad kinam baz ne?
where will the beast lead me?

(repeat)

----- story development -----

dômo ad mam itkunz akkraz
I try to go back home again

mônar i afêkz kubatu
but no one recognizes me

suro i sisi hal mok
my eyes are like stars now

dômo eba i dinbôz mok
my body has been reborn

----- (repeat pre-chorus) -----
----- (repeat chorus) -----
----- story development -----

numz! numz! ad seda dô numz!
run! run! run into the fields!

ruskank urilz dô dimok
the zotcengs are following you now

erem i tranzum uid dimok
you color is no longer hidden

ad balim temz as tamaz
you must protect each other until death

----- (repeat chorus) -----
----- (repeat last story development) -----
----- (repeat chorus) -----
