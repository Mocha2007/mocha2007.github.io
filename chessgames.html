Chess Game Comparison

AI strength is determined by how long it takes Stockfish 11 (Depth: 22) to mate the AI. Unless otherwise stated, the most difficult AI is chosen. The titles are sorted by implementation correctness, then AI strength, then alphebetically. Implementation errors are sorted from most to least problematic. If a game is marked as green, I have tested that all rules are followed. Things checked for:

  • castling (kingside and queenside)
  • en passant
  • underpromotion
