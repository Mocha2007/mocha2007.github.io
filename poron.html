Porön Language

Porön is the language spoken in Porön, a region north of the Muran languages.

N-Mutation

N-mutation mutates consonants to their corresponding nasal of the same place of articulation, and nasalizes vowels. For <h>, it simply becomes [n].


The following remarks apply to all dialects:
  • Final <e> is /ə/

Morphology

  • The plural is formed by adding an -e to the end of a noun or adjective (which may instead trigger y-mutation), and verbs are pluralized via n-mutation.

Affixes

  • -e / -y = forms the plural of most nouns and adjectives
  • -n = forms the plural of most verbs
  • -n = is a "collection" affix affixed to plural nouns, treated grammatically singular

Grammar

  • Verbs conjugate to number and tense.
