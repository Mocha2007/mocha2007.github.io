Snowclones

When I encounter a snowclone not in other databases, I put it here. Snowclones, for the uninitiated, are genericized copypastas, and are probably an intermediate stage to becoming 'official' irregular grammatical constructions. They are similar to madlibs, but the words are deliberately chosen and the snowclone often carries an effectual meaning. Additionally, snowclones are often, if not always, derived from popular quotes.
