Ur

I have also created a program, if you wish to play this game.
  • m>n - a piece in position m moves to position n (15 tiles total)
  • mxn - a piece in position m captures a piece in position n
  • Ø - forced non-move.
  • ...&... - Landed on a lotus, second move.
  • ...+ - Point for player.
  • ...# - Point for player, game over.

Dicerolling Odds

  • 0 -\(\frac 1{16}\)
  • 1 -\(\frac 14\)
  • 2 -\(\frac 38\)
  • 3 -\(\frac 14\)
  • 4 -\(\frac 1{16}\)
