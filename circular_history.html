Circular History

Here is a list of all cyclical models of history, or comparisons between past and present histories. In parentheses, I also note the approximate length of the hypothesized cycle.

  • American Empire - Comparison of America to the rise and fall of Rome (c. few centuries)
    • Written in 2003 - None of the three predicted parallels came to pass, so maybe there's hope?
    • Alternatively - You have a century and a half before worrying about becoming a dictatorship, so plan accordingly
  • Dynastic Cycle - Mandate of Heaven and the rise and fall of Chinese dynasties (14-554 years)
  • Morphological Typology Cycle - Morphological Typology as a 4-cycle (c. few millenia)
  • Strauss–Howe generational theory - Generations as a 4-cycle (71-110 years)
