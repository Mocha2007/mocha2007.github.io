Orthography

It uses 5 "consonant" characters, and 15 "vowel" characters. The vowels are just /a e i o u/ w/ or w/o /w/ and /j/ at the beginning. The consonants are /t s k h N/.

Grammar

  • Either SOV or OSV.
  • Literally no pronouns.
  • Three determiners for "this/that/yon" (this by me, that by you, yon far from us).
  • Adjectives come before and after a noun, mostly corresponding to before = es. ser and after = es. estar
Object classes
  • Linear - stick, feather, pen, ...
  • Planar - ground, person, ...
  • Compact - sphere, cube, stump, ...
  • Liquid - water, milk, ...
  • Intangible - concepts, ideas, ...
