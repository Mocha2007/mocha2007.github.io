Sound Changes.

Spirantization

  • Tuscan Gorgia - /p t k/ → /ɸ θ h/ if /V_/

Voicing

  • Verner's Law - /ɸ θ s x xʷ/ → /β ð z ɣ ɣʷ/

Vowels

  • Brugmann's Law - /o/ → /a/ but sometimes /ā/

Unsorted

  • Grimm's Law - eg. /dʰ/ → /d/ → /t/ → /θ/
  • High German Consonant Shift - eg. /p/ → /f/ if /_#/ else /pf/; /d/ → /t/
