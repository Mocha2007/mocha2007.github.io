Even/Odd Number Properties

Axioms:
  1. The sum, difference, and product of two integers is also an integer
Definitions:
  1. An even integer n is an integer that can be expressed as n = 2k where k is an integer.
  2. An odd integer n is an integer that can be expressed as n = 2k+1 where k is an integer.
Theorems:
  1. Theorem: the sum of two even integers is even
    1. let m, n be even integers of the form m = 2a and n = 2b
    2. m+n = 2a+2b
    3. m+n = 2(a+b)
    4. a+b is an integer by axiom 1
    5. since m+n can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer, m+n is also even
  2. Theorem: the sum of two odd integers is even
    1. let m, n be odd integers of the form m = 2a+1 and n = 2b+1
    2. m+n = 2a+1+2b+1
    3. m+n = 2(a+b+1)
    4. a+b+1 is an integer by axiom 1
    5. since m+n can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer, mn is even
  3. Theorem: the sum of an odd and even integer is odd
    1. let m, n be odd and even integers of the form m = 2a+1 and n = 2b
    2. m+n = 2a+1+2b
    3. mn = 2(a+b)+1
    4. a+b is an integer by axiom 1
    5. since m+n can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer plus 1, mn is odd
  4. Theorem: the product of two even integers is even
    1. let m, n be even integers of the form m = 2a and n = 2b
    2. mn = (2a)(2b)
    3. mn = 2(2ab)
    4. 2ab is an integer by axiom 1
    5. since mn can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer, mn is also even
  5. Theorem: the product of two odd integers is odd
    1. let m, n be odd integers of the form m = 2a+1 and n = 2b+1
    2. mn = (2a+1)(2b+1)
    3. mn = 4ab + 2a + 2b + 1
    4. mn = 2(2ab + a + b) + 1
    5. 2ab + a + b is an integer by axiom 1
    6. since mn can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer plus 1, mn is also odd
  6. Theorem: the product of an odd and even integer is even
    1. let m, n be odd and even integers of the form m = 2a+1 and n = 2b
    2. mn = (2a+1)(2b)
    3. mn = 4ab + 2b
    4. mn = 2(2ab + b)
    5. 2ab + b is an integer by axiom 1
    6. since mn can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer, mn is even
  7. Theorem: the square of an even integer is even
    1. n = 2a
    2. n2 = (2a)2
    3. n2 = 4a2
    4. n2 = 2(2a2)
    5. 2a2 is an integer by axiom 1
    6. since n2 can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer, n2 is even
  8. Theorem: the square of an odd integer is odd
    1. n = 2a+1
    2. n2 = (2a+1)2
    3. n2 = 4a2 + 4a + 1
    4. n2 = 2(2a2 + 2a) + 1
    5. 2a2 + 2a is an integer by axiom 1
    6. since n2 can be expressed as the product of 2 and an integer plus 1, n2 is odd
