Hiragana/Katakana Tips/Mnemonics

  • To remember the order of the kana:
    • A Kind Samurai Told Naomi How My Yak Ran Wild (+n)
    • HAIL UNESCO
  • To distinguish the hiragana nu (ぬ) and me (め):
    • Imagine nu as a pair of chopsticks (first stroke and beginning of the second stroke) grabbing some noodles (loop at end of stroke 2).
    • ...and me just doesn't have any noodles, I guess.
  • To distinguish the katakana tsu (ツ) and shi (シ):
    • the ten for tsu are aligned to the top of the character: ツ
    • the ten for shi are aligned to the side of the character: シ
