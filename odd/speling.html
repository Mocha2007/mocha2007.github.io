* - unstressed

Word-finally, and before R or NG, unmacronized vowels represent the sound of their macronized forms, except u/ū - eg. tre represents /e/, not /ɛ/, and er represents /e/, not /ɛ/. The only exception to this is ONG, which represents /ʌŋ/ as expected.
