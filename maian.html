Maian Language

Maian is the language spoken on the moon Maia.

Phonology

There are five consonants, and zero vowels. Syllable Structure is SF. Stress is on the first syllable.

Grammar

  • SOV order.
  • Only nouns and verbs... yeah.
  • The default word form is noun.
  • Compoundedness is always marked with hyphens, for clarity.
  • The -tt suffix forms one-arg verbs. The suffix -ph forms two-arg verbs.
